About Bio Coal - Briquettes

Looking to reduce your carbon footprint? Make the switch to Biocoal and get ready for a greener future. Our eco coal briquettes are made from agricultural waste, providing an effective and sustainable way to generate energy. Join thousands of happy people who've already made the switch and start enjoying cleaner energy today!

Why Us

Laboratory Tested

At Bhoomi every batch of Eco-coal is tested at authorized laboratory for calorific value and moisture content. In order to improve the clients’ efficiency we aim to deliver highest calorific value and lowest moisture content briquettes.
