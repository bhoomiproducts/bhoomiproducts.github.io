About Eco Coal

Eco Coal or Bio Coal Briquetting is the process of converting agricultural waste into high density and energy concentrated fuel Briquettes. Efficiency

Bio Coal Efficiency over Coal

Why Us

Laboratory Tested

At Bhoomi every batch of Eco-coal is tested at authorized laboratory for calorific value and moisture content. In order to improve the clients’ efficiency we aim to deliver highest calorific value and lowest moisture content briquettes.

Advantages

Customized Products Low Transportation Cost Reliability

Best Quality

We are committed to our clients to deliver highest calorific value and lowest ash content ‘Eco-coal’ and to achieve this quality monitoring is done at each step of the manufacturing process. At Bhoomi we have devised three step quality monitoring process

R&D

We believe that innovation is the only way of achieving efficiency and effectiveness simultaneously. Our Research and Development Team continuously look for new technologies and innovations in briquetting process from all around the world and incorporate them in our manufacturing process.
